SEOLOGY.AI©

We were fixing SEO manually for 30 clients. Then we automated it.

We were sending SEO audit reports with 47 issues found. Clients paid us $3,000/month. Three weeks later, we'd send the same report—43 issues remaining. New developers kept breaking what we fixed.

One night at 2 AM, tired of manually fixing the 18th missing meta description that month, we asked: what if AI just logged into the CMS and fixed it? Six months later, SEOLOGY.AI was born.

Our Mission

SEO shouldn't require a full-time person. We're building AI that handles it automatically—no spreadsheets, no Slack pings, no monthly retainers.

Automation Over Reporting

We don't just tell you what's broken. We fix it. Every tool before us sent reports—we deploy changes to your live site automatically.

Zero Manual Work

Connect once. Never touch it again. Our AI monitors your site 24/7 and applies fixes the moment issues appear—no human intervention required.

Built for Scale

Whether you manage 1 site or 1,000, SEOLOGY.AI scales with you. Agencies use us to automate SEO for hundreds of clients simultaneously.

Our Journey

Jan 2024

The Breaking Point

After fixing the same 200 meta tags for the 4th time in a month (across 30 client sites), we realized agencies don't scale because SEO doesn't scale. We started building an AI that could log into any CMS and fix issues autonomously.

Mar 2024

First Live Fix

Our AI successfully connected to a Shopify store and fixed 47 missing alt tags without human intervention. The client called us: "Did you just... fix everything automatically?" Yes. Yes we did.

Jul 2024

Closed Private Beta

Launched with 50 hand-picked agencies and SaaS companies. They connected 247 sites. Our AI deployed 18,392 fixes in the first month. Zero false positives. Zero broken sites.

Nov 2024

Public Launch

SEOLOGY.AI goes public with support for Shopify, WordPress, and custom sites via Magic.js. First platform in history that automatically fixes SEO issues instead of just reporting them.
