Work Process

01

.Connect Store

Connect your Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce store via OAuth in 60 seconds. Our AI instantly scans every product page, category, collection, and variant—detecting missing meta descriptions, poor image alt text, duplicate titles across product variants, and schema errors.

02

.Generate Product SEO

AI analyzes each product's title, description, category, price, and attributes to generate unique, keyword-optimized meta tags. For a "Men's Running Shoes - Blue" product, we create: "Buy Men's Blue Running Shoes | Free Shipping Over $50" with a conversion-focused description that includes size, color, brand, and benefits.

03

.Deploy to Storefront

With one click, changes go live on your storefront. Product schema markup (price, availability, reviews) appears in Google Shopping results within 48 hours. All changes are reversible with automatic rollback—we never break your store.
