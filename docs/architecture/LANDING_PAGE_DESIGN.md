## 15. DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Palette
**Primary:**
- Blue: #3B82F6 (blue-600)
- Blue Gradient: from-blue-600 to-purple-600

**Background:**
- Dark: #111827 (gray-900)
- Medium: #1F2937 (gray-800)
- Subtle: #374151 (gray-700)

**Text:**
- Primary: #FFFFFF (white)
- Secondary: #D1D5DB (gray-300)
- Tertiary: #9CA3AF (gray-400)

**Accents:**
- Success: #10B981 (green-500)
- Warning: #F59E0B (amber-500)
- Error: #EF4444 (red-500)

### Typography
**Headlines:**
- Font: Inter, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont
- H1: 3.5rem (56px), bold, line-height 1.1
- H2: 2.5rem (40px), bold, line-height 1.2
- H3: 1.75rem (28px), semibold, line-height 1.3

**Body:**
- Font: Inter
- Large: 1.25rem (20px), normal, line-height 1.6
- Regular: 1rem (16px), normal, line-height 1.6
- Small: 0.875rem (14px), normal, line-height 1.5

### Spacing
- Section Padding: py-20 (80px vertical)
- Container Max Width: max-w-7xl (1280px)
- Grid Gap: gap-8 (32px)
- Button Padding: px-8 py-4 (32px x 16px)

### Animation Guidelines
**Scroll Animations:**
- Fade in + slide up on scroll (intersection observer)
- Stagger children by 100ms delay
- Duration: 600ms, easing: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)

**Hover States:**
- Scale: scale(1.05)
- Duration: 200ms
- Add subtle shadow on hover

**CTA Buttons:**
- Pulse animation on hover
- Gradient shift on hover
- Box shadow depth increase

## 16. MOBILE CONSIDERATIONS

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px (1 column)
- Tablet: 640-1024px (2 columns)
- Desktop: > 1024px (3+ columns)

### Mobile-Specific Changes
**Hero:**
- Stack CTA buttons vertically
- Reduce headline from 7xl to 4xl
- Hide or simplify hero visual

**How It Works:**
- Vertical stepper instead of horizontal
- Larger tap targets (min 44px)

**Feature Grid:**
- 1 column on mobile
- Card-based layout with full-width cards

**Pricing:**
- Horizontal scroll cards
- Or stack vertically with "Most Popular" first

### Performance
- Lazy load images below fold
- Defer non-critical CSS
- Preload critical fonts
- Target: < 2s LCP, < 100ms FID

## 19. CONTENT TONE & VOICE

### Brand Voice Guidelines

**Confident, Not Cocky**
‚úÖ "We fix your SEO automatically"
‚ùå "We're the only SEO tool you'll ever need"

**Technical, Not Jargon-Heavy**
‚úÖ "Claude AI analyzes your site and generates fixes"
‚ùå "Our NLP-powered semantic analysis engine leverages transformer models"

**Benefit-Focused, Not Feature-Dumping**
‚úÖ "Stop spending $3,000/month on manual SEO work"
‚ùå "Includes 47 different SEO checks and validators"

**Honest, Not Hyperbolic**
‚úÖ "Most customers see results in 30 days"
‚ùå "Guaranteed #1 rankings in 24 hours!"

### Writing Style
- Active voice ("We fix issues" not "Issues are fixed")
- Short sentences (max 20 words)
- Contractions welcome ("We're" not "We are")
- Address user as "you"
- Avoid: "leverage," "synergy," "solutions," "ecosystem"

This landing page design focuses on:

1. **Immediate Clarity:** Headline makes unique value obvious
2. **Problem ‚Üí Solution:** Show pain, then relief
3. **Trust Building:** Social proof, security badges, testimonials
4. **Multiple CTAs:** 9 conversion opportunities throughout page
5. **Platform Proof:** Show it works with their stack
6. **Risk Reversal:** Free trial, no CC, 90-day rollback
7. **Mobile-First:** Optimized for all devices

**Key Differentiators Highlighted:**
- "Fixes, not just reports"
- "Actually logs into your CMS"
- "Powered by Claude AI"
- "Three execution modes"
- "90-day rollback"
